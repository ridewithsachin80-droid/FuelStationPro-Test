---

### 2. Smart Alerts ⭐⭐⭐
**Before:** Reactive management  
**After:** Proactive prevention

**Monitors automatically:**
- Low fuel (< 500L) → Alert every 30 min
- High cash (> ₹50k) → Alert every 15 min  
- Unclosed shifts (> 2 hours) → Alert every hour
- Unusual sales (> ₹10k) → Alert immediately

**Sends WhatsApp to:**
- Owner (low fuel, unusual sales)
- Manager (high cash, unclosed shifts)
- Employee (shift summary)

---

### 3. Auto-Save Drafts ⭐⭐
**Before:** Lost data on browser crash  
**After:** Never lose work again

**How it works:**
- Saves form data every 5 seconds
- Stores in browser (offline capable)
- Restores on page reload
- Shows "Draft saved" indicator
- Clears after submit

**User sees:**
"You have unsaved work from 5 minutes ago. Continue?"

---
